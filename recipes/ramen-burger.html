Ramen Burger

Description

This recipe is designed to mimic to famous burger from Smorgsburg. It combines Asian cuisine with American by incorporating ramen noodles as the bun with a beef patty in the middle.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of instant ramen
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt & pepper for taste
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 slices American cheese
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha
  • 1 1/2 cups arugula
